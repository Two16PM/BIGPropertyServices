Our Services for Your Investment Property

At Two 16, we provide a personalized and proactive approach to property management, ensuring that your investment is managed with the utmost care and efficiency. Our services are designed to simplify property ownership and enhance the value of your investments, with a focus on personalized service, proactive management, and transparent pricing.

1. Full-Service Property Management

Our comprehensive management service is tailored to meet your specific needs, ensuring that every aspect of your property is handled with care:

  • Customized Property Management: Experience hands-on management that treats your property as if it were our own.
  • Personalized Pricing:
    • Monthly Management: 10% of rental income.
    • Setup Fee: $300 one-time, with a personalized onboarding process.
    • Leasing and Listings: Tenant placement and property listing for half a month’s rent, using targeted marketing strategies.
    • Lease Renewals: $200 per renewal, including proactive lease renewal strategies to retain quality tenants.
    • Maintenance: 10% management fee for repairs and upkeep, with a focus on preventative maintenance to avoid costly repairs.
    • Rent Collection & Accounting: Seamless rent collection and transparent financial reporting.
    • Evictions: No additional eviction fees, handled with expertise to minimize disruption.
    • Late Fees: $50 (paid by the tenant).
    • Tenant Retention: Proven strategies and strong communication to reduce vacancies and retain high-quality tenants.

2. Leasing Only Services

For property owners who prefer to manage their properties themselves but need expert tenant placement:

  • Targeted Tenant Placement: Property listing and tenant placement for half a month’s rent, with a focus on finding the right tenant for your property.
  • Comprehensive Tenant Screening: In-depth background checks to ensure that you attract reliable and responsible tenants.

3. Property Inspections

Ensure your investment is well-protected with our detailed inspection services:

  • Thorough Property Assessments: Our inspections help identify potential issues and ensure your property is in top condition.
  • Inspection Types:
    • Pre-Purchase Inspections: Detailed inspections before buying a property, providing peace of mind and informed decision-making.
    • Turnover Inspections: Conducted during tenant turnovers or as needed for property assessment, to ensure the property is well-maintained.
  • Pricing:
    • Up to 2500 sq ft: $800.
    • Over 2500 sq ft: $900.
  • Same-Day Reports: Receive timely, detailed reports to quickly address any issues.
