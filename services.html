Our Services for Your Investment Property

At CIRCLE, we provide a personalized and proactive approach to property management, ensuring that your investment is managed with the utmost care and efficiency. Our services are designed to simplify property ownership and enhance the value of your investments, with a focus on personalized service, proactive management, and transparent pricing.

1. Full-Service Property Management

Our comprehensive management service is tailored to meet your specific needs, ensuring that every aspect of your property is handled with care:

  • Customized Property Management: Experience hands-on management that treats your property as if it were our own.
  • Monthly Management: 10% of rental income.
  • Setup Fee: $300 one-time, with a personalized onboarding process.
  • Leasing and Listings: Tenant placement and property listing for half a month’s rent.
  • Maintenance: 10% fee for repairs and upkeep with your approval.
  • Evictions: No additional eviction fees.

2. Leasing Only Services

For property owners who prefer to manage their properties themselves but need expert tenant placement:

  • Targeted Tenant Placement: Property listing and tenant placement for half a month’s rent.
  • Comprehensive Tenant Screening: In-depth background checks to ensure reliable tenants.

3. Property Inspections

Ensure your investment is well-protected with our detailed inspection services:

  • Thorough Property Assessments: Identify potential issues and ensure your property is in top condition.
  • Pre-Purchase Inspections: Detailed inspections before buying a property.
  • Turnover Inspections: Ensure the property is well-maintained during tenant turnovers.
  • Same-Day Reports: Receive timely, detailed reports to quickly address any issues.
